Body Shape Guide

5 Body Shape Types

  • Rectangle: Similar measurements throughout, athletic or lean build
  • Inverted Triangle: Broad shoulders with narrow waist, athletic build
  • Triangle: Narrow shoulders with wider midsection
  • Oval: Rounded midsection with narrower shoulders and hips
  • Trapezoid: Well-proportioned with balanced silhouette

T-Shirt Fit Comparison

Slim Fit

Tailored close to the body with modern silhouette. Best for rectangle and inverted triangle shapes.

Regular Fit

Classic straight cut offering comfortable all-day wear. Versatile for most body types.

Athletic Fit

Designed for muscular builds with room through chest and arms. Perfect for athletic body types.

Relaxed Fit

Roomier cut for maximum comfort. Ideal for triangle and oval body shapes.

Oversized Fit

Deliberately loose and baggy for contemporary streetwear look. Great for making a style statement.

Longline Fit

Extended length with contemporary styling. Modern proportions for layering.
