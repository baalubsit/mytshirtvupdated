T-Shirt Measurement Guide

Follow our comprehensive guide to find your perfect fit. Proper measurements ensure your custom t-shirt looks and feels great.

How to Measure

1

Chest Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal. For shirts, measure 2 inches below armpits.

2

Length Measurement

Measure from the highest point of shoulder to desired length (usually waist level).

3

Sleeve Length

Measure from shoulder seam to desired sleeve end point.

4

Shoulder Width

Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across the back.

Sizing Tips

For Best Fit

Compare your measurements to our size chart rather than relying on your usual size, as sizing can vary between brands.

Between Sizes?

If you're between sizes, consider your preferred fit. Size up for a looser fit, or size down for a more fitted look.

Fabric Consideration

Our cotton tees may shrink slightly after washing. Consider this when choosing your size.
